Solder Stencil PCB Holder

Stencil holders allow you to line up and apply solder paste to your PCBs perfectly every time.

Enter the dimensions of your PCB to download the corresponding STL file.

Is your PCB not a rectangle, or is an odd size? Download the STEP file to customize in your CAD package of choice.

Web app and OpenSCAD model by Curly Tale Games, credit to Michael Graham on Thingiverse for designing the original STL.
